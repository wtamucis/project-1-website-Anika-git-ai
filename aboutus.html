About Us

The Coastal Bistro is more than just a restaurant, it’s a dream brought to life. Our goal was never just to serve food, but to create a space where good meals, family, and community come together by the water.

We’re a family-owned coastal restaurant built on love, hustle, and a whole lot of flavor. When we stumbled upon a quiet little spot by the shore, we didn’t just see a building, we saw the start of something special. Together, we rolled up our sleeves, put in the work, and opened the doors to what would become The Coastal Bistro.

Everything on our menu has a story. Our Cajun Chicken Pasta is a family favorite. The shrimp tacos are legendary (trust us, you’ll come back for them), and our key lime pie? That’s the kids' proud creation. We believe food tastes better when it's made with heart, and shared with the people you love.

Here’s why guests keep coming back:

  1. Fresh, local ingredients sourced sustainably from the coast.
  2. Scenic waterfront views to enjoy while you dine.
  3. A welcoming, family-friendly atmosphere where everyone feels at home.

Whether you're stopping in after a day at the beach or looking for your new favorite local spot, we hope you feel right at home here.
